About Us

Welcome to Hotel Himalaya, a luxury hotel in the heart of Kathmandu. We offer a range of rooms and suites to meet the needs of business and leisure travelers alike. Our hotel features stunning views of the Himalayas and is located just minutes away from popular tourist destinations such as Durbar Square and Swayambhunath.

At Hotel Himalaya, we believe that every guest deserves the best possible experience. That's why we offer a range of amenities including an outdoor pool, a fitness center, and a spa. Our on-site restaurants serve up delicious local and international cuisine, and our friendly staff is always on hand to help you make the most of your stay.
